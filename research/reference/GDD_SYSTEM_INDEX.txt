   - Health System (hidden 0-100 composite score, visual state indicators)
   - Pests & Disease (varroa, SHB, chalkbrood, nosema, AFB, EFB)
   - Frame Inspection & Laying (brood pattern, queen laying algorithm)

   - Player XP Track (5 levels, unlock tiers and recipes)

   - Skill Trees (Genetics, Health, Extraction tracks)
   - Quests & Events (story, task, seasonal, challenge types)
   - NPC Chains (multi-part per NPC, relationship building)

================================================================================
CRITICAL NUMBERS & FORMULAS
================================================================================

QUEEN PERFORMANCE:
- Laying rate peak: 1,500-2,000 eggs/day (Year 2, S-grade, healthy)
- Grade modifier: +25% (S) to -100% (F)
- Age multiplier: ×1.05 (Year 2 peak) → ×0.20 (Year 6+)
- Grade degradation: S→A (18 mo), A→B (24 mo), B→C (36 mo), etc.

POPULATION LIFECYCLE:
- Worker lifespan: 6 weeks (spring/summer), 4-6 months (winter)
- Larval development: 3 days (egg) + 6 days (larva) + 12 days (capped) = 21 days
- House bee phase: 12 days internal work, 3-4 days transition
- Forager phase: 5-8 weeks external work
- Base mortality: 0.1-3% daily by stage/season

ENERGY SYSTEM:
- Max: 100
- Passive drain: 1-2/hour standing
- Tasks: 1-3 (very light) to 25-40 (very heavy)
- Sting cost: 5-8 per sting
- Restoration: full sleep (100), meal (40-50), temp effects (15-25)

EQUIPMENT CONDITION:
- Range: 0-100
- Degradation: -1 (maintained/shed) to -4 (unprotected) per week
- Impact threshold: below 50 = -10% production, -5% survival chance
- Repair: basic (+20), advanced (+65), craftable (+65)

HARVEST MECHANICS:
- Frame sizes: deep 3,500 cells, super 2,450 cells
- Weight per frame: 5 lbs (deep full), 3.5 lbs (super full)
- Moisture grades: <18% (excellent), 18-21% (good), >24% (poor)
- Winter reserve minimum: 60 lbs (warning below 40 lbs)
- Grading affects price: $12-14 (premium) to $4-5 (poor)

DISEASE MECHANICS:
- Varroa threshold: 1 mite per 100 bees triggers treatment
- Contagion: 5% per day between hives at same location
- AFB: colony death, equipment destroyed
- EFB: treatable with requeening or antibiotics
- Equipment sharing increases contagion significantly

FORAGE & PRODUCTION:
- Peak summer forage: 6,000+ lbs potential per location
- Early spring forage: 1,500 lbs potential per location
- Low forage impact: -20% population growth, stagnation
- Diverse forage: better honey grades, multi-color blends
- Seasonal multiplier: spring 1.0x, summer 1.5x, fall 0.5x, winter 0x

STING PROBABILITY:
- Base chance: 10% (calm) to 25% (defensive) per inspection step
- Bee suit modifier: ×0.20 (80% reduction)
- Smoke modifier: ×0.85 (15% reduction)
- Combined: ×0.17 (95% total reduction)
- Evening modifier: ×1.25 (foragers returning)

COLONY STRESS MODIFIER:
- Baseline: 1.0x laying rate
- Increases: congestion (×1.15), food shortage (×1.20), disease (×1.25), queenless (×1.50)
- Decreases: strong forage (×0.85), adequate space (×0.95), healthy queen (×0.90)
- Can reduce laying rate 40-60% if multiple stressors stack

================================================================================
SYSTEM DEPENDENCIES & INTEGRATION
================================================================================

Hive Health depends on:
- Population count (strong/weak/none)
- Brood pattern quality (solid/spotty/failing)
- Disease/pest presence (varroa, chalkbrood, etc.)
- Winter food stores adequacy
- Equipment condition (>75 good, <30 poor)
- Time since last inspection
- Swarm preparation status

Honey Production depends on:
- Queen laying rate (driven by grade, age, health, stress)
- Forage availability (location + season + weather)
- Colony population (forager count)
- Hive health (good health = higher efficiency)
- Time (takes 21 days to develop first adults)
- Equipment condition (<50% causes -10% production)

Queen Genetics depend on:
- Breeding selection (player chooses which queens to breed)
- Virgin queen mating success (DCA minigame, genetic roll)
- Grade inheritance (mother's grade ±1 letter)
- Age tracking (degrades over 2-3 years)
- Disease susceptibility (inherits varroa tolerance, chalkbrood resistance)

Player Progression depends on:
- XP earning from actions (inspections, harvests, quests)
- Knowledge accumulation (skill tree unlocks)
- Economic success (cash from honey sales funding expansions)
- NPC relationships (quests unlock, prices improve)
- Seasonal progression (summer = production, winter = planning)
