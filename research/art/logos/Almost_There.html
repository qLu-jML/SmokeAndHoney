Base: Nathan's 2A Iteration, Option D — ornate amber script, smoke wisp curling from the S, dark warm-brown background, bee smoker below the text.
Three required fixes applied to all 8 variants:  ① Smoker redrawn as proper bellows smoker (tin cylinder + leather accordion bellows + nozzle spout).  ② The O in "Honey" replaced with a hexagonal honeycomb cell.  ③ Honey drips removed entirely.

What to keep vs. what to fix from Option D

KEEP — Typography
Ornate/decorative amber pixel art script. "Smoke" large on top, small ampersand "&" in the middle, "Honey" below. Warm golden-amber fill with dark brown outlines and pixel highlight dots. Slight stagger/overlap between words for energy.
KEEP — Color & Background
Dark warm brown background (#2a1608 range). Letters in golden amber (#E6A220) with warm orange-brown shadows. Smoke wisp in light gray-white curling from the top of the S. Overall palette: amber, honey, dark mahogany.
KEEP — Smoke Wisp
A single stylized smoke curl rising from behind or above the S in "Smoke." Soft, wispy, gray-white, 2-3 pixel-art puffs. Integrates visually with the word rather than floating disconnected.
FIX — Smoker
Currently: a plain gray barrel. Needed: proper bee smoker — upright tin cylinder body (silver-gray), brown leather accordion bellows pump on the left side, thin metal nozzle spout at the top angled forward, visible rivet detail on the cylinder, smoke wisps rising from nozzle.
FIX — O in Honey
Currently: round O letterform. Needed: hexagonal honeycomb cell replacing the O — flat-top hex outline in the same amber gold as the lettering, amber honey fill inside, slightly darker wax-border hex edges. Should read as both an O and a comb cell.
FIX — Honey Drips
Currently: small amber drip shapes hanging off letters in "Honey." Remove entirely. No drips, no liquid decoration on any letter. Keep the letters clean and dry.
