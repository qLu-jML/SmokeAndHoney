Weight Methodology

Four reviewers provided feedback on NPC sprite options (A/B/C/D) for front-facing (portrait/shop) and top-down (overworld) views. Weights were assigned per Nathan's instructions and normalized to 100%.

ReviewerRaw WeightNormalizedRole

Raw total: 200. Each weight divided by 2 to normalize to 100%. Consensus is determined by summing normalized weights for each option pick.

For each NPC below, you'll find the weighted consensus (which option won) with specific refinement notes, plus a ready-to-paste Leonardo prompt. The prompts incorporate the base style tags from the project's prompt library and fold in every reviewer's feedback proportional to their weight.
