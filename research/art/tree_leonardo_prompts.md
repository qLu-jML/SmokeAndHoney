# Leonardo AI Tree Sprite Prompts
## Settings: Lucid Origin model, 16:9 aspect ratio, Dynamic style

All prompts follow the same pattern that worked for the Willow:
- 4 seasonal variants side by side
- 3/4 view isometric-adjacent perspective
- No text labels, no ground shadow/dirt
- Pixel art style, clean outlines
- Beige/cream solid background (for easy removal)

---
