# Smoke & Honey - Game Design Document

> **Smoke & Honey** is a beekeeping simulation where you learn by looking. You inherit a single hive on a quiet Iowa homestead, and through weekly inspections, seasonal rhythms, and hard-earned intuition, you grow from a nervous beginner into a master beekeeper.
