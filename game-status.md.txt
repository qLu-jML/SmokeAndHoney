# Smoke & Honey — Current Game Status
## Last Updated: April 4, 2026

## What The Game Is
Smoke & Honey is a cozy-but-deep beekeeping farm sim built in Godot 4. 
The player inherits a single Langstroth hive on a quiet Iowa homestead 
and learns real beekeeping through observation, seasonal rhythms, and 
consequence. Set in Cedar Bend, a fictional small town in Iowa, the game 
spans five in-game years with a full cast of characters, quests, and a 
central emotional narrative.

## Current Build Version
v0.2 — Core simulation complete, Year 1 playable loop in progress

## Completed Features
- Full beehive simulation (colony populations, queen behavior, varroa, disease)
- Hive inspection system (multi-box, frame-by-frame, cell tooltips)
- Day/night cycle, 8-month calendar, 4 seasons, weather system
- Equipment shop (Feed & Supply), Saturday Market (sell honey to Frank)
- Smoker, hive tools, storage chest, hive management
- Colony installation (package bees and nuc)
- Varroa treatment (alcohol wash, oxalic/formic acid)
- American Foulbrood disease model with hive destruction
- Barrel feeder and feed stores system
- Quest system with 5 Year-1 quests implemented
- Knowledge Journal (16 entries + hive records)
- Character creator (name, appearance, pronouns, backstory)
- Save/load system
- 7 exterior zones + 7 interior scenes with transitions
- Music system (8 seasonal tracks)
- Seasonal NPC dialogue (Bob, Ellen, Frank)
- Full flower lifecycle system (7 species, seasonal quality)
- Modular hive visuals (7 components, build-phase visuals)
- Community Standing system with price multipliers

## In Progress
