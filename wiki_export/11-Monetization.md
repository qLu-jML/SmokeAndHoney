# Monetization (TBD)


Smoke & Honey is a premium, buy-once title. No pay-to-win. No energy timers. No required internet connection. All gameplay-critical content ships with the base game. Paid add-ons, if any, are cosmetic or additive (new regions with unique flora and bee species) — never gating.


| PC (Steam) | Premium one-time purchase, ~$20–25. Paid DLC expansions for new regions/climates (each with unique bee species and flora). |
| --- | --- |
| Mobile (iOS/Android) | Free-to-play core with optional cosmetic Season Pass. All gameplay-critical content is free. Paid content: hive themes, apiary skins, special event access. |
| Shared Principles | No pay-to-win. No energy timers. No mandatory ads. Progression is earned through play. Any paid content is cosmetic or additive, never gating. |
